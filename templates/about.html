About

This web application was created to help both people, companies and banks alike.

By creating, training and deploying a Machine Learning model, this web app is able to predict how much risk an individual represents when applying for a credit based on different parameters such as:

  • Gender.
  • Annual Income.
  • Number of days someone has been employed.
Depending on the submitted information, the model will yield a determined outcome.

After the evaluation of these parameters, the user will know if they represent a Low Risk, Medium Risk or a High Risk when submitting an application.

To know your risk level, please head on over to the Quiz page to begin.
